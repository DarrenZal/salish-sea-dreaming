# Salmon Forest Cycle

- **Category:** Paintings
- **Artist:** Briony Penn
- **Filename:** IMG_1478.jpg
- **Description:** A watercolor illustration depicting the nutrient cycle between salmon and the coastal rainforest.
  - **River/Stream:** Spawning salmon (likely Chum or Coho with distinct spawning coloration) are swimming upstream. Their carcasses and eggs are visible on the riverbed, providing nutrients.
  - **Forest:** Bears (Grizzly or Black Bear) are feeding on salmon at the river's edge. The nutrients from the salmon are implied to feed the large Sitka Spruce or Cedar trees in the background.
  - **Vegetation:** Lush undergrowth with ferns and berries (salal or huckleberry).
- **Notes:** Illustrates the "salmon forest" concept where marine nutrients support terrestrial ecosystems.
