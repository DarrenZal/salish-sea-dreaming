# Salish Sea Ecosystem Vertical

- **Category:** Paintings
- **Artist:** Briony Penn
- **Filename:** IMG_1473.jpg
- **Description:** A detailed vertical watercolor composition illustrating the Salish Sea ecosystem from the sea floor to the surface and coast.
  - **Underwater (Bottom):** Features a large red octopus, various shellfish (clams, scallops), rockfish, eelgrass beds, and divers conducting surveys.
  - **Mid-water:** Shows herring schools, salmon, and a killer whale (orca) dorsal fin.
  - **Surface/Coast:** Depicts a BC Ferries vessel, a "SLOW" buoy with cormorants resting on it, a traditional indigenous canoe, shoreline birds (herons, ducks), and a coastal landscape with trees and distant mountains.
- **Notes:** likely a draft or component for a larger educational map or poster.
