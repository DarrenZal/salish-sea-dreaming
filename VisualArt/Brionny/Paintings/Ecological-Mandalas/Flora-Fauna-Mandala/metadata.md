# Flora & Fauna Mandala

- **Category:** Paintings / Mandalas
- **Artist:** Briony Penn
- **Filename:** IMG_1485.jpg
- **Description:** A circular watercolor "mandala" composition featuring a diverse array of plants, birds, and insects native to the Salish Sea region.
  - **Species depicted:** Includes Common Camas, Chocolate Lily, Shooting Star, various ferns, lichens, a woodpecker (Downy or Hairy), a hummingbird, a towhee, and other songbirds.
  - **Text:** The names of the species are written in calligraphy along the spokes and rim of the wheel.
- **Notes:** likely represents a seasonal phenology wheel or an ecosystem web.
