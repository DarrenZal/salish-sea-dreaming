# Garry Oak Meadow Mandala

- **Category:** Paintings / Mandalas
- **Artist:** Briony Penn
- **Filenames:** IMG_1486.jpg, IMG_1488.jpg
- **Description:** A circular watercolor composition focused specifically on the Garry Oak meadow ecosystem.
  - **Species depicted:** Garry Oak tree (leaves/branches), Camas (Common and Great), Fritillaria (Chocolate Lily), Sea Blush, Blue-eyed Mary, Shooting Star, Satin Flower, Nootka Rose, Snowberry, Osoberry.
  - **Insects:** A ProperTi's Duskywing butterfly (Erynnis propertius) is featured, which relies on Garry Oak ecosystems.
  - **Detail:** IMG_1488.jpg provides a close-up view of the Duskywing butterfly and the surrounding calligraphy.
  - **Text:** Calligraphy names each plant and includes brief phenological notes (e.g., "blooms for April Fools").
- **Notes:** Educational piece highlighting the biodiversity of endangered Garry Oak ecosystems.
