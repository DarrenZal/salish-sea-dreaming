# Kelp Forest Ecosystem

- **Category:** Paintings
- **Artist:** Briony Penn
- **Filename:** IMG_1477.jpg
- **Description:** A split-view watercolor showing the relationship between seabirds and the underwater kelp forest.
  - **Above Water:** Marbled Murrelets (or similar alcids) are shown swimming on the surface and diving, with one flying in the air. The background features a forested coastline.
  - **Below Water:** A rich kelp forest scene. A diving bird is pursuing fish underwater. A large rockfish (possibly a Copper or Quillback Rockfish) is prominent in the foreground amongst colorful anemones, sea stars, and urchins.
- **Notes:** Highlights the connection between the marine canopy (kelp) and avian predators.
