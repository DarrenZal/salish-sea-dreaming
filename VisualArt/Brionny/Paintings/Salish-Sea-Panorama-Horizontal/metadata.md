# Salish Sea Panorama Horizontal

- **Category:** Paintings
- **Artist:** Briony Penn
- **Filename:** IMG_1476.jpg
- **Description:** A horizontal panoramic watercolor showing a cross-section of the Salish Sea environment.
  - **Left (Underwater):** Depicts a schooling bait ball of herring, salmon, a basking shark feeding with mouth open, and a grey whale or humpback whale in the background.
  - **Right (Surface/Land):** Shows a coastline with forested hills, a rocky beach with oystercatchers and crows, and various seabirds (gulls, murres) flying or floating. A boat is visible in the distance.
- **Notes:** Comprehensive ecosystem view, connecting deep water species with the shoreline.
