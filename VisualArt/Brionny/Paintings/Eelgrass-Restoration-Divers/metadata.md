# Eelgrass Restoration Divers

- **Category:** Paintings
- **Artist:** Briony Penn
- **Filename:** IMG_1475.jpg
- **Description:** Watercolor scene depicting two scuba divers working in an eelgrass meadow.
  - **Action:** The divers appear to be planting or surveying eelgrass. One is using a light or tool.
  - **Environment:** Lush green eelgrass beds with visible roots and substrate. Herring roe is visible on the eelgrass blades. Large crabs (Dungeness or Red Rock) are in the foreground.
  - **Details:** A rope line runs down from the surface, possibly an anchor line or transect line.
- **Notes:** Illustrates marine conservation and restoration fieldwork.
