## If a wall goes dark (most likely problem)

This usually means the projectors lost the display handshake overnight. **The fix is one click inside Resolume.**

1. Go to the computer (monitor + keyboard at the tower).
2. Find the **Resolume Arena** window on screen (orange "A" icon in taskbar if minimized).
3. In Arena's top menu bar, click **Output → Advanced Output**.
4. A panel opens. **Just opening it is the fix** — walls should come back within 5–10 seconds.
5. Close the panel. Done.

**If walls do not come back within 30 seconds → call Prav.**

## Autoloom — full restart (only if Prav asks)

If Prav asks you to bring Autoloom back up, follow these steps **in order**:

1. On the desktop, double-click the **AutoLoom** icon.
2. When it opens, click **AutoLoom Live**.
3. Click **Start**.
4. In the model dropdown, choose **`000120.pickle`**.
5. **Wait** until the "network snapshot" text appears (can take 30–60 seconds).
6. Click **Animate**.
7. Set **speed to `0.185`**.

That's the whole sequence. If any step doesn't look right, stop and call Prav.
